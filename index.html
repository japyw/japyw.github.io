Let me tell you more about my journey into technopreneurship.

I've been interested in business and tech since I was a child. When I got my first hand-me-down laptop at 7, I would come home and play games non-stop and read up about many things on the Internet. Even now, almost 2 decades later, I still do the same. To me, technology is anything that solves your problems or improves your life, be it fire, a vehicle, or air-conditioning. My passion for tech and business led me to continuously start my own businesses and project. I've been grinding non-stop at my own ideas since I was 10 (with varied success), and I don't intend to stop.
